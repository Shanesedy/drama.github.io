Shane Vincent Sedlemyer is an academic practitioner of Theatre. He from a small town in Western Pennsylvania named Nicktown. He has his MA in English Literature from the Indiana University of Pennsylvania, and is currently in the English Literature and Criticism Program there where he is a Graduate Assistant in the Digital Humanities.

He recieved his Bachelors from Penn State University in which he was trained in Theatre, Music, and Dance. There, he worked on several shows in various disciplines.
