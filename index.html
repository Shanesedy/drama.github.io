General Bio

Shane Vincent Sedlemyer is an academic practitioner of Theatre. He has his MA in English Literature from the Indiana University of Pennsylvania, and is currently in the English Literature and Criticism Program there.

He recieved his Bachelors from Penn State University in which he was trained in Theatre, Music, and Dance. There, he worked on several shows in various disciplines.
